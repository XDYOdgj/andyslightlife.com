Elevate Your Timepiece with Stylish Replacement Clock Hands

These replacement clock hands are crafted to enhance the appearance of your quartz clock, offering a sleek and modern look. Perfect for serpentine style clock movements, the black and white design creates a striking contrast that brings sophistication to any room. The durable aluminum construction ensures long-lasting performance without compromising on style.

Perfect Fit for Your Timepiece

Designed for dials ranging from 8 1/2" to 9", these hands are engineered to fit seamlessly into your clock's structure. The set includes 3 pieces, offering versatility for different clock sizes and styles, making it easy to match your timepiece with the perfect look.

The black and white design not only makes it easy to read time but also adds a sense of elegance and refinement. The second hand features a red tip, positioned 3 1/2" from the center, while the minute hand extends 4 1/4" from the center, ensuring precise and functional placement.

Easy to Install and Fast to Ship

These clock hands are simple to install, requiring minimal effort to replace your old ones. Plus, they are shipped quickly, so you can enjoy the updated look of your clock in no time.
