LITEBAND ACTIV 520 – Ultra-Bright LED Headlamp for Adventure and Work

Light up your path with the LITEBAND ACTIV 520, engineered for precision and comfort. Featuring a 210° wide angle LED, this headlamp delivers clear, broad illumination without the need to constantly adjust your head. Whether you're hiking, running, or working in the field, the ACTIV 520 offers reliable, high-quality lighting that stays with you through every challenge.

Key Features

  • 210° Wide Angle LED Headlamp – Ensure complete visibility with this ultra-bright, wide-coverage light source.
  • Comfy Low-Profile Design – Engineered to be lightweight and comfortable for all-day wear.
  • 5 Modes: High, Med, Low, Pulse, Green – Switch between brightness levels and specialized lighting as needed.
  • Rechargeable Lithium Ion Battery (1800mAh) – Stay powered with long-lasting, eco-friendly battery life.
  • Adjustable Expansion Band – Easily fits a wide range of hard hats and helmets for a secure, customized fit.

Performance at Its Best

With 20 high-quality LEDs and a powerful 520 lumens output in high mode, the LITEBAND ACTIV 520 is built for high-end performance. Its compact 4.9-ounce size makes it easy to carry, perfect for any situation where reliable lighting is essential. The NO-BOUNCE light distribution ensures even illumination, keeping your wearables comfortable and secure throughout all your activities.

Perfect for Every Adventure

Whether you're exploring the great outdoors or working long hours in challenging environments, the LITEBAND ACTIV 520 is your trusted lighting companion. Its adjustable band, ultra-bright LED, and durable design make it ready for every task.
