PHLIPESAM Rechargeable Headlamp - Bright, Adjustable, and Waterproof

PHLIPESAM Rechargeable Headlamp

Light up your adventure with a powerful, versatile, and durable headlamp built for the outdoors.

Unmatched Brightness & Power

Powered by a high-capacity 1200mAh battery, the PHLIPESAM headlamp delivers up to 1000 lumens of brightness, making it perfect for exploring in the dark. From moonlit trails to early morning hikes, this headlamp ensures you have clear visibility no matter the time of day.

10 Advanced Light Modes for Every Situation

Whether you're camping, reading, or signaling for help, the PHLIPESAM headlamp offers 10 customizable modes, including high, low, red, and strobe. This flexibility allows you to tailor your lighting to match the demands of your adventure, from casual strolls to intense outdoor challenges.

Waterproof & Rugged Design

Equipped with an IP66 waterproof rating, this headlamp is built to thrive in tough weather conditions. From rain to snow, you can rely on it to keep functioning and protecting your vision wherever you go.

Hands-Free & Adjustable

Thanks to built-in motion sensor technology, you can operate the headlamp with your hands free, ideal for tasks like cooking, climbing, or riding. The 120° adjustable angle lets you direct the light exactly where you need it, making this headlamp an essential tool for runners, hikers, and campers.

Detachable & Portable

The detachable design makes it easy to carry the headlamp separately, whether you're on the move or looking to save space in your gear. It's a great companion for both solo and group adventures, giving you freedom and flexibility in every situation.

  • 1000 Lumens
