Coast XPH25R Dual Power Headlamp with 410 Lumen Brightness

Experience Unmatched Brightness with the Coast XPH25R Headlamp

Whether you're hiking through dense forests, camping under the stars, or preparing for an emergency situation, the Coast XPH25R 410 Lumen Rechargeable Dual Power Headlamp is designed to keep you safe and illuminated. This versatile headlamp combines powerful lighting with the latest innovation to meet your on-the-go needs.

Intelligent Lighting Solutions

Featuring the Coast Pure Beam Twist Focus system, the XPH25R allows you to adjust the beam effortlessly between short and long-range illumination without encountering hotspots or dimming. This feature ensures the perfect light for every situation, from close-up tasks to scanning the horizon.

Dual Power Flexibility

The dual power system offers the convenience of using either ZITHION-I or ZITHION rechargeable batteries, giving you a reliable and cost-effective lighting solution. Plus, the option to use alkaline batteries provides backup when you need it most. This flexibility ensures you're always prepared, no matter the conditions.

Built for the Field

Constructed with a durable, dust-resistant design, the XPH25R is built to withstand the toughest outdoor adventures. Its robust build means you can rely on it in challenging environments without worrying about damage or performance loss.

Convenience at Your Fingertips

The built-in magnetic base makes it easy to convert this compact headlamp into a powerful handheld flashlight. Simply detach and use it as needed, and the real-time power indicator ensures you know how much battery life you have left, giving you full control over your lighting experience.

Key Features:

  • 410 Lumen Max Light Output
  • Dual Power System - Rechargeable & Alkaline Batteries
  • Up to 393ft (120m) Max Beam Distance
  • 9.25 hr Max Runtime
  • Twist Focus Beam Adjustment

Ideal For:

Hiking, camping, outdoor adventures, and emergency use. This headlamp is your reliable partner in any field situation, combining performance, durability, and convenience in one compact device.

Why Choose the Coast XPH25R?

With a magnetic base and interchangeable batteries, the Coast XPH25R is designed for adaptability and long-lasting use. Whether you're exploring the wild or need lighting during an unexpected situation, this headlamp delivers everything you need to stay safe and productive.
